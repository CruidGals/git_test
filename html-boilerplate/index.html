How to bake a apple-pumpkin smash pie

  1. Get a pumpkin and an apple
  2. Smash both of those until you have them into a nice fine paste
  3. Mix them together with seasoning of your choice
  4. Bake at 350 degree farenheit, make sure it is preheated
